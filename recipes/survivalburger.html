Survival Burger

A convenient snack for the explorer.

The Survival Burger is easy to make and customizable to your taste, making it a go-to option for a quick and filling meal on the go!

This recipe yields one serving of Survival Burger, which is a filling and nutritious meal for a busy day in Stardew Valley. Feel free to adjust the ingredients to your liking, or add some extra condiments like ketchup or mayo to enhance the flavor.

    Game Ingredients:

  • Bread (1)
  • Cave Carrot (1)
  • Eggplant (1)

    Instructions:

  1. Begin by cracking the egg and mixing it with the wheat flour to create the burger patty.
  2. Cut the beet, common mushroom, cave carrot, and spice berry into small pieces and cook them in a pan until they are soft and tender.
  3. Slice the tomato and lettuce and set them aside for later.
  4. Once the vegetables are cooked, add them on top of the burger patty in the pan.
  5. Let the mixture cook for a few minutes until the patty is fully cooked and the vegetables are warmed through.
  6. Add the cheese on top of the patty and vegetables, and let it melt.
  7. Place the cooked patty on a bun, and add the sliced tomato and lettuce on top.
  8. Serve hot and enjoy!

Tom Kha Soup

These flavours are incredible!

Tom Kha Soup is a delicious and comforting meal that will transport your taste buds to the sunny shores of Thailand!

This recipe yields about 2-3 servings. Feel free to adjust the recipe quantities to suit your taste preferences.

    Game Ingredients:

  • Coconut (1)
  • Shrimp (1)
  • Common Mushroom (1)

    Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, combine the coconut milk and chicken broth and bring to a simmer over medium heat.
  2. Add the lemongrass, kaffir lime leaves, and ginger to the pot and let it simmer for 5-10 minutes to allow the flavors to infuse into the broth.
  3. Add the chicken slices and mushrooms to the pot and cook for another 5-7 minutes or until the chicken is fully cooked.
  4. Stir in the fish sauce, sugar, and lime juice to the pot, adjusting the seasoning to taste.
  5. Remove the lemongrass stalk and kaffir lime leaves from the pot and discard.
  6. Serve the soup in individual bowls, topped with cilantro and sliced Thai bird chilies (if desired).

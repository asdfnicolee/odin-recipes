Pink Cake

There's little heart candies on top.

Stardew Valley Pink Cake is a delicious dessert made with a moist and fluffy melon-flavored cake.

This recipe yields 8-10 servings depending on how large you slice the cake!

    Game Ingredients:

  • Melon (1)
  • Wheat Flour (1)
  • Sugar (1)
  • Egg (1)

    Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease a 9-inch cake pan with butter or cooking spray.
  2. In a medium-sized mixing bowl, combine the flour, baking powder, and salt. Stir well and set aside.
  3. In a large mixing bowl, beat the butter and sugar together until light and fluffy, using an electric mixer or a stand mixer fitted with a paddle attachment.
  4. Add the eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition.
  5. Gradually add the flour mixture to the butter mixture, alternating with the milk, starting and ending with the flour mixture. Beat well after each addition.
  6. Add the melon puree and beat until the batter is well combined and pink in color.
  7. Pour the batter into the prepared cake pan and smooth the top with a spatula.
  8. Bake for 30-35 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.
  9. Remove the cake from the oven and let it cool in the pan for 10 minutes.
  10. Serve the Pink Cake as is or with your favorite frosting or whipped cream. Enjoy!
